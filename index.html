Unbiased and Second-Order-Free Training for High-Dimensional PDEs

ICML 2026
Jaemin Seo, Su Rin Lee, Jae Yong Lee†
Department of AI, Chung-Ang University
†Corresponding Author
Paper Code arXiv

Abstract

We propose Un-EM-BSDE, an unbiased and second-order-free training framework for BSDE-based PDE solvers. Our method eliminates Euler-Maruyama discretization bias without requiring Hessian computations, achieving accuracy comparable to unbiased baselines while maintaining training efficiency similar to EM-BSDE.

Method

Un-EM-BSDE eliminates discretization bias by forming independent error groups and multiplying their averages, yielding an unbiased estimator without second-order derivatives.

Results

Un-EM-BSDE achieves accuracy comparable to Heun-BSDE and FS-PINNs while maintaining training time similar to EM-BSDE (1.79× vs 42.91× and 32.07×).

Conclusion

Un-EM-BSDE removes discretization-induced bias without requiring second-order derivatives, achieving strong accuracy while maintaining computational efficiency comparable to EM-BSDE.
